    CHAPTER 06 · BUG REPORTS

    Report a bug.

    Found something broken in the simulator? Send it our way — every report gets read by the developer. Screenshots are gold; the more detail you give us, the faster we can fix it.

    We automatically include your browser, screen size, language, and current page so you don't have to type it out. If you opened this from inside the simulator, your game state (mode, team, turn) is also captured.

    What's a good bug report?

    The reports we fix fastest are the ones that give us enough to reproduce the issue. A few things that help:

    • What you were doing when it broke — "tried to play a match against Iceland after starting a World Conquest run as England"
    • What you expected to happen vs what actually happened
    • A screenshot — even a phone photo of the screen works. Visual context catches things text descriptions miss.
    • Whether it's reproducible — happens every time, or just once?

    What if I don't get a reply?

    We read every report but we don't reply to every one. If you've left an email and your bug is critical (game-blocking, data loss, security), we'll follow up within a few days. Smaller issues get folded into the next release without a reply.

    If your issue is more of a feature request than a bug, head to our Contact page — we have a separate path for those.
