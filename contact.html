    CHAPTER 05 · GET IN TOUCH

    Contact us.

    Football Conquest is a small project, but it's a real one — we read every message. Here's how to reach us depending on what you need.

    Email

    The fastest way to reach us for anything that isn't a public comment: contact@footballconquest.com.

    We aim to respond within 2-3 business days. Messages sent over the weekend usually get a reply by Tuesday.

    YouTube

    If you'd rather leave a public comment or message the channel directly:

    • @Ghiellini — for English-language viewers. Career-mode and simulation videos. Best place for general questions, feature ideas, and community discussion.
    • @GRKN7 — Türkçe izleyiciler için. Kariyer modu ve simülasyon videoları. Genel sorular, özellik fikirleri ve topluluk sohbeti için en iyi yer.

    Comments on recent videos get the most eyeballs. DMs work too, but please don't send multiple in a row — we see them.

    Bug reports

    If something in the simulator is broken, the more detail you give us, the faster we can fix it. A useful bug report includes:

    • What you were doing when it broke (e.g. "tried to play a match against Iceland after starting a World Conquest run as England")
    • What you expected to happen
    • What actually happened
    • Your browser and OS (e.g. "Chrome 124 on Windows 11")
    • A screenshot if you can — even a phone photo of the screen helps

    Send these to the email address above with the subject line starting with [BUG] and we'll route it straight into the fix queue.

    Feature requests

    We keep a running list of community-suggested features and rotate the most-requested ones into each new release. If you have an idea, the best path is:

    1. Email us with subject [FEATURE], or
    2. Leave a comment on a relevant YouTube video, or
    3. Reply to the announcement video for the latest release.

    Things we've actually shipped from community requests so far: the Hide UI hotkey, the FALLEN-in-Battle roulette, Save and Load campaigns, and the 10-language interface.

    Press and partnerships

    If you write about indie games, sports tech, or content-creator tools and want to cover Football Conquest, please reach out at contact@footballconquest.com with subject line [PRESS]. We can provide:

    • High-resolution screenshots and the brand mark
    • Background on the format, the simulator, and the audience it serves
    • Direct quotes from the developer for attribution
    • Early access to upcoming features for review embargo timing

    For brand collaborations, sponsored content, or affiliate partnerships, send a deck or an outline — be specific about audience overlap and what kind of integration you're proposing. We respond to every legitimate inquiry, but please understand we're selective about partnerships that match the editorial voice.

    Creator requests

    If you make Football Conquest videos and want your run featured on our Featured Runs page, send the video link to contact@footballconquest.com with subject [FEATURE-MY-RUN]. We review every submission. Criteria we look for:

    • The simulator is clearly visible (not just used briefly as a clip)
    • Original commentary or editing — not just gameplay footage
    • A complete run, or at minimum a strong arc with multiple turns
    • The format is respected — no skipping the FIFA-rated outcome, no fake scores

    Tagging #FootballConquest in your video description makes it easier for us to find your work even if you don't email.

    What we don't do

    To save everyone time, a quick list of things we get asked about regularly but won't be doing:

    • Custom development for hire — we don't take freelance simulator-customization projects.
    • Source code sales or licensing — the simulator's source is not for sale or sublicensing.
    • FIFA account or coin trading — we have nothing to do with the EA Sports FC marketplace.
    • Tournament hosting — we're not an esports organization. If you want to run a Football Conquest tournament with your community, you don't need our permission.
    • Match outcome predictions — the simulator generates probabilistic results from FIFA ranking points. Don't use it for betting.

    About data and privacy

    If your question is specifically about data handling, cookies, or what we collect — please start by reading our Privacy Policy. If your question isn't answered there, email us at the address above with subject line [PRIVACY] and we'll respond within 7 days, in line with general data-protection norms.

    For European Economic Area (EEA) and California (CCPA) data requests, use the same subject line. We'll handle requests within the timeframes those regulations require.

    One more note

    Football Conquest is run by one person with a YouTube channel and a small but loyal community. Replies might not be instant. They will be real — every email gets read by the developer, not a support bot. Thanks for being patient with that, and for caring enough about the project to write in.
