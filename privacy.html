    Privacy Policy.

    Last updated: May 14, 2026

    Introduction

    This Privacy Policy describes how Football Conquest ("we", "us", "the Site") collects, uses, and shares information about visitors to footballconquest.com. By using this Site, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

    Information we collect

    We collect limited information from visitors, primarily through third-party services. Specifically:

    • Automatically collected data: When you visit the Site, your browser sends certain information automatically including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit.
    • Game data: The simulator stores some preferences locally in your browser (such as your light/dark mode choice and creator settings). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
    • We do not collect: We do not ask for or store names, email addresses, payment information, or any personally identifying details. There is no account system on this Site.

    Cookies and similar technologies

    This Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your device. Cookies are used for:

    • Essential cookies: Required for the Site to function, such as remembering your simulator settings.
    • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site (which pages are popular, how long people stay, what device they use). This information is aggregated and anonymized.
    • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense and its partners to serve ads relevant to your interests across the web.

    You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but parts of the Site may not function correctly.

    Google AdSense and advertising

    This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet.

    You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you may opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

    For more information about Google AdSense, see Google's advertising policies.

    Google Analytics

    This Site uses Google Analytics, a service provided by Google, Inc., to analyze how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information including pages visited, time spent, device type, and approximate geographic location. This data helps us improve the Site.

    You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

    Third-party content

    This Site embeds content from third parties including YouTube (for video embeds) and Google Fonts. When you interact with embedded content, those third parties may collect data about you according to their own privacy policies.

    Children's privacy

    This Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

    Data retention

    We do not directly retain personal data. Data collected by Google Analytics is retained according to Google's data retention settings (default: 14 months). Data collected by Google AdSense is governed by Google's policies.

    Your rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information including the right to access, correct, or delete data collected about you. Since we do not directly store personal data, most requests should be directed to Google for data collected through Analytics and AdSense.

    If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including the right to object to processing and the right to data portability.

    If you are located in California, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

    Changes to this policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

    Contact

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us through our YouTube channel: @Ghiellini.
